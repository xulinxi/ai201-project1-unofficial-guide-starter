COMMENTS:

[score 1] Chill out, don't overcommit, explore your interests. Unstructured time is good. Take Social Dance with Richard Powers.

How to make friends:
- Keep your door open
- Say yes to things
- Hang out in common spaces
- Don't let your social anxiety win
- Clubs are nice
- Ask people if they want to do things with you

[score 1] spend as little time on schoolwork as possible. for maximum efficiency, know that (if you do STEM) there tend to be two types of TAs.

1) TAs that are good at explaining concepts but probably won't give you the answer. you should attend their discussion section

2) TAs that are terrible at explaining but will just give you the answer. you should attend their office hours to just get the PSET answers

[score 1] Which one do you prefer: TAs that give answers or help you understand?
